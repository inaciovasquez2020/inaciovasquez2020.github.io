The Expander Obstruction and Logic-Width Dependency

Inacio F. Vasquez

This paper characterizes the fundamental failure of $FO^k$ local types to bound global structural invariants in spectral expanders. We resolve this by establishing the Logic-Width Dependency $k \ge f(tw)$, proving that for bounded-treewidth systems, local homogeneity forces global rigidity.
