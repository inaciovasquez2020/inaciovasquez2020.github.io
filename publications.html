The Expander Obstruction and Logic-Width Dependency

Inacio F. Vasquez

This paper characterizes the fundamental failure of \(FO^k\) local types to bound global structural invariants in spectral expanders. We resolve this by establishing the Logic-Width Dependency \(k \ge f(tw)\), proving that for bounded-treewidth systems, local homogeneity forces global rigidity.

Finite Capacity and Rigidity in Operator Systems

Inacio F. Vasquez

An exploration of URF rigidity bounds applied to finite operator systems. We demonstrate how spectral gap preservation ensures deterministic capacity retention in homogeneous structures, bridging the gap between operator algebra and information topology.

Vasquez Research Program
