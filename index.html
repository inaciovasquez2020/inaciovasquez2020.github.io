Inacio F. Vasquez

Independent Researcher — Foundations of Physics & Mathematics

Abstract. Structural and information-theoretic approaches to physical law, with emphasis on locality, computation, and operational constraints. This program develops unified rigidity frameworks spanning deterministic, probabilistic, and informational regimes.

Research Program

The research agenda investigates how finite information capacity, locality, and structural invariants impose universal limits on physical dynamics, computation, and inference.

Core Frameworks & Repositories

  • Cycle–Local Rigidity (FO⁴): Structural locality and overlap rigidity in bounded-degree graphs; finite machine-verifiable certificates.
  • Unified Rigidity Framework (URF Core): Axiomatic system for deterministic, probabilistic, and averaged coercivity regimes.
  • Chronos / EntropyDepth: Information-theoretic lower bounds on refinement depth and computational time.
  • Operational Wavefunction Collapse: Information-centric resolution of measurement dynamics.
  • Exchange Force Framework: Structural treatment of exchange interactions.
  • Radiative Rigidity: Universal constraints on long-distance radiative corrections.
