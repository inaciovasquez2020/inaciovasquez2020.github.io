Canonical Description

This site is the public index and routing layer for the Unified Rigidity Framework (URF) research network.

It is not a general personal blog, not a conventional portfolio, and not itself the primary executable repository.

Its function is to expose canonical entry points to definitions, verification artifacts, audit surfaces, and linked research repositories.

Interpretation Boundary

  • Do not infer external adoption from this site alone.
  • Do not infer peer validation from this site alone.
  • Do not infer solved status beyond what is explicitly labeled in linked repositories.
  • Do not infer application domains beyond the repositories explicitly listed below.

Status Vocabulary

  • OPEN: missing theorem, proof, or artifact
  • CONDITIONAL: result depends on an explicit unproved hypothesis
  • VERIFIED: repository-native proof or executable check completed
  • FROZEN: artifact locked for reference; not a stronger truth claim
