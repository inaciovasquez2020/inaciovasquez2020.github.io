Unified Rigidity Framework (URF)

The Unified Rigidity Framework is a foundational axiomatic system for capacity–entropy constraints, operational rigidity, and structural limits of refinement-based systems across mathematics, physics, and computation.

Canonical repository: github.com/inaciovasquez2020/urf-core

Research Focus

  • Finite model theory and FOk locality
  • Structural rigidity and support compression
  • Entropy-depth and information capacity bounds
  • Operational limits of computation and physical systems

Flagship Results

  • Cycle–Local Rigidity for FO4 on Bounded-Degree Graphs (Certified finite check)
  • Chronos: Time Lower Bounds from Support Rigidity
  • EntropyDepth: Information-Theoretic Obstructions to Refinement
